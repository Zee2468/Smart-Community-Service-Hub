Welcome to Smart Community Hub

Smart Community Hub is an innovative digital platform developed to strengthen communication between community members and local authorities.

Residents can report service delivery issues, monitor progress, receive important notifications, participate in community events, and discover employment opportunities, all from one centralized platform.

Our Vision

To build smart, connected and sustainable communities where citizens actively participate in improving service delivery through technology, transparency and collaboration.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide an easy-to-use platform that allows residents to report community issues, track progress, access opportunities, receive notifications and stay informed about community development initiatives.
